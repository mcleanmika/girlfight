GET INVOLVED IN YOUR COMMUNITY

WHAT IS GIRL FIGHT?


Girl Fight is an app directed at and for woman. A way to become more involved in their community with events that influence and or motivate them. Events such as protests, information evenings, as well as events to raise money for things such as breast cancer awareness.



HOW IT WORKS


The app uses your location to find events near you. You are able to use the search engine to filter events or use the map to look around and see what is happening in your community. Your login will have all your upcoming events saved into your calendar in order to plan and organise. As a member you are able to create your own event to be posted on the map pending approval.






SAFETY & PRIVACY


In order to ensure the safety of all members. Members must first be verified. All events will be approved through the Girl Fight team. In order to gain information on an event, you again must be verified. When creating an event you are able to set the event settings to private or an open event. Open events will be posted on the map, however closed events mean that you will have to be invited by the admin.
